## Design Context

### Users
Grace Notes serves people doing daily reflection in a personal, low-pressure routine. The core job is to capture gratitude, needs, and people-in-mind with minimal friction, then return later for meaningful review. Sessions should feel focused and calm rather than demanding.

### Brand Personality
Voice and tone should be calm, warm, encouraging, and supportive. The experience should feel trustworthy and gentle, not pushy or performative. Preferred personality words: calm, warm, supportive.

### Aesthetic Direction
Primary direction is light-mode-first, with warm paper-like surfaces, soft contrast, and clear reading comfort. Visual reference leans toward Headspace-style emotional softness while keeping journaling structure legible. Avoid looking gamified, corporate dashboard-like, or social-feed noisy.

Current product signals to preserve:
- Warm neutral background and paper surfaces
- Muted text with a warm accent
- Rounded containers and low-friction controls
- Serif-forward typography for reflective reading tone

### Design Principles
1. Keep reflection calm: prioritize visual quiet, whitespace, and low cognitive load.
2. Encourage without pressure: celebrate progress gently, avoid aggressive urgency cues.
3. Favor clarity over novelty: strong hierarchy, readable typography, predictable interactions.
4. Preserve emotional safety: avoid gamification and attention-hacking patterns.
5. Stay practical for daily use: optimize for quick entry flows and effortless revisit.
